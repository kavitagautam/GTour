Trip Overview

Duration:15 Days Max Altitude:6,461m Group Size:2 to 12 People Best Seasons:March-May & Sep-Nov

Mera Peak stands at the height of 6,461M and its summit offers mesmerizing views of Mt. Everest, Kanchenjunga, Makalu, Lhotse, Chooyu and other mountains. Mera Peak climbing is an adventurous Peak Climbing with technical difficulties and it requires good physical fitness. The climbing beginning from Hinku Valley can be wonderful. The beautiful landscapes, rare flora and fauna along the way will make it a heavenly experience.
